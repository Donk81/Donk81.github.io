Beginner Disc Golf Tips

Start with the basics: simple rules, safe form, and easy practice routines.

First Round Checklist

  • Bring at least one putter and one midrange disc.
  • Wear comfortable shoes with good traction.
  • Pack water and check the weather before you go.
  • Play with a friend who has played before, if possible.

Simple Throwing Tips

  1. Focus on smooth, controlled form instead of power.
  2. Keep the disc flat and release at chest height.
  3. Follow through toward your target.
  4. Practice short throws before trying max distance.
